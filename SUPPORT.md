## 🛠 Before You Post

Before opening a new request, please perform these three "Quick Checks":

1.  **Search existing Issues/Discussions:** Someone may have already solved your problem.
2.  **Check the Documentation:** View our [Getting Started Guide](link) or [FAQ](link).
3.  **Update to Latest:** Ensure you are running the latest version of the project.

---

## 📝 Asking for Help

When you do post, providing the following information helps us help you faster:

* **Environment:** (e.g., Node version, OS, Browser)
* **Context:** What were you trying to achieve?
* **Logs/Screenshots:** Copy-paste the exact error message or a screenshot of the UI.
* **Minimal Reproducible Example:** If it's a bug, can you provide a small snippet of code that triggers the issue?
